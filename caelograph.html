Caelograph



Naming, printing campaign and print documentation.

This is one my first important work as a graphic designer. It begin with a new writing instrument limited edition design by Caran d'Ache. "The first writing instrument link to the celestial vault, the laws of astronomy and the history of time ."

The agency I was working for was commissioned to develop the entire communication on this project. For my part I had so much fun looking for a name, thinking "anyway, they'll never used on of mine". And I was wrong, they chose one of my proposals and here born "Caelograph". I also work on the main campaign visual, and design the press release and the owner's guid book.
  • Client: Caran d'Ache
  • Project Scope: Art Direction, Illustration
