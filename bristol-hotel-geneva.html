"Voyage au Bristol"



Save the date, invitation & brochure

For its re-opening, Bristol Hotel Geneva organized a private party. The aim of it, was to present the new interior design. While looking for an appropriate theme, I thought that "Travel" would be the most efficient and declinable way to illustrate the concept. 

So the Save the date became a postcard, the Invitation a boarding pass and the Brochure a personalized passport.
