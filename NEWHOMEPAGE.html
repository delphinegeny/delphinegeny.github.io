My name is Delphine. I was born in France, in the pretty little town of Annecy.
Since I always been in love with Italy after a history licence, a visual art degree and a 3 years working experience as a graphic designer I packed up my suitcases and moved to Italy where I passed a Master degree in Art direction at Politecnico di Milano.

The experience made me into a bit of a nomad, where, after graduating I had the opportunity to work in agencies and company located in Rome and Geneva. I am currently in Bologna where I have been living and working as a freelance art director for one year and a half.

With extensive experience designing for a vast array of industries, working for both advertising agency and company, I am capable of seeing a creative projects from concept through to production.
I consider myself to be a problem-solver when it comes to creative direction and execution, plus I have a heightened attention to detail.

As I’m convinced that diversity and cultural exchange is the key of creativity and success I never miss an occasion to travel, meeting people and discover new cultures. I like to be surrounded by truly inspiring people, who are passionate about what they do, who are hungry to achieve more and who can help me grow in ways I couldn’t imagine.
