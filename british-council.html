Print Campaign

English is now essential and used every day. Lots of italian people are still thinking that learing english is too difficult for them. We want to encourage them to feel confident with english showing that understanding and communicating in English is within everyone’s reach.

Insight : THANK TO BRITISH COUNCIL ENGLISH IS NOT FAR AWAY ANYMORE.

We decide to show this proximity involving 3 italian cities to some of the most famous monuments of London city. Il claim L’inglese è di casa could be translate as to be a part of the family, to feel confident with english. This typical italian expression emphasize how much the two languages and cultures are close and connecting today. The objective of the campaign is to demostrate that thanks to the British Council italian people can feel more confident with english language, like if they were at home or close to it.
