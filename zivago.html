Zivago



Brochure and printing campaign

In celebration of the 56th anniversary of the Nobel price Boris Pasternak, for his legendary work, Doctor Zhivago, the Maison Caran d'Ache has created a new limited edition writing instrument.

It was kind a challenge for me to deal with this huge story and to match historical and cultural context of the novel and the writing instrument design by Caran d'Ache. For the visual identity I prefered keep the 3 colors of the writing instrument ( black, white and gold) and play with the paper and the stamping process.
  • Client: Caran d'Ache
  • Project Scope: Art Direction, Illustration
  • Agency: Markette, Geneva
