Le Poinçon de Genève

Le Poinçon de Genève



Official Certificate

As a young graphic designer it was a chalenge but also a privilege to deal with an historical and official document such as this certificate.

My mission was to update the old version adding a unique authentication key and a QR code, design and made using a unique process that combines hologram and hot stamping.
Some other nicety was, for the "Poinçon" logo to match the embossing one with the both hot stamping logo on both sides of the certificate.
  • Client: Timelab, Foundation – Geneva Laboratory of Horology and Microengineering
  • Project Scope: Art Direction
  • Agency: Markette, Geneva
