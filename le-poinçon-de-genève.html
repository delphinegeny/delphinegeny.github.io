Le Poinçon de Genève

Le Poinçon de Genève



Official Certificate

As a young graphic designer, it was a challenge but also a privilege for me, to deal with such a historical and official document as this certificate.

My mission was to update the old version, adding a unique authentication key and a QR code, designed and made by using a unique process that combines hologram and hot stamping. Another peculiarity was that the "Poinçon" logo matched the embossing one with both the hot stamping logo on the two sides of the certificate. 
  • Client: Timelab, Foundation – Geneva Laboratory of Horology and Microengineering
  • Project Scope: Art Direction
  • Agency: Markette, Geneva
