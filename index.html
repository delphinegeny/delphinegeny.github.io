Things that I do

Key area of experience include art direction and senior level design in print, digital, identity, branding, brand management, packaging and typography.




Some Clients:

Caran d'Ache, Baume & Mercier, Quilvest, 88 Rue du Rhone, British Council, Land Rover, Poste Italiane, Nivea, Hotel Bristol, Grand Hotel Kempinski, Cotril, Martel.


My name is Delphine. I born in Annecy a pretty little town located in the south of France. Since I always been in love with Italy after a history licence, a visual art degree and a 3 years working experience as a graphic designer I packed up my suitcases and moved to Italy. I graduated cum laude at Politecnico di Milano, where I gained a Master degree in Art direction and copywriting.
The experience made me into a bit of a nomad, where, after graduating I had the opportunity to work for agencies and company located in Rome and Geneva. I am currently in Bologna where I have been living and working as a freelance art director for one year and a half.



With extensive experience designing for a vast array of industries, working for both advertising agencies and companies, I am capable of seeing a creative projects from concept through to production. I consider myself to be a problem-solver when it comes to creative direction and execution, plus I have a heightened attention to detail.

As I’m convinced that diversity and cultural exchange is the key of creativity and success I never miss an occasion to travel, meeting people and discover new cultures. I like to be surrounded by truly inspiring people, who are passionate about what they do and who are hungry to achieve more.
