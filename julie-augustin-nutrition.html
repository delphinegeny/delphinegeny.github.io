Julie Augustin Nutrition



Identity & Website

Working on this visual identity was kind of a challenge for me: to represent the perfect balance between medical utility and personal service easily accessible to everyone.

I was looking for an essential style at the same time simple, serious and especially able to inspire confidence. For the website I decided to work with Rachel Korinek, an australian photographer. I am a fan of her photos, which, while being refined, always have a story to tell.

  • Photographer: Rachel Korinek
