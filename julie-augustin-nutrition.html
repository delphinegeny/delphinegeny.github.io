Julie Augustin Nutrition



Identity & Website

Working on this visual identity was kind of a challenge for me: to represent the perfect balance between the medical and personal value of this service, which is easily accessible to everyone.

I was looking for an essential style both simple, elegant and, above all, with the ability to convey confidence. For the website, I decided to work with Rachel Korinek, an australian photographer. I am a fan of her refined photos, that always have a story to tell.

  • Photographer: Rachel Korinek
