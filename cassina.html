Advertising campaign

As a homage to Charlotte Perriand, Cassina and Louis Vuitton designed a limited edition of the iconic Chaise Longue Le Corbusier LC4.

The product became the main protagonist of the campaign. To strengthen the link between the savoir-faire of the two Maison, the illustration and the insight is inspired of fashion Haute-Couture sketch.
