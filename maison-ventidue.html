Website

Mariarosa Lamana ask me for a new website for her organisation. Maison Ventidue is a match between a "private house" and an exhibition and performance space.

As they are doing with art they wanted to be able to play with their website and blog. For this reasons we look for a clean and accessible design that would be easy to update and leave all the creativity to art project displayed on the site.
